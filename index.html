Call of Cthulhu

Resources for our Call of Cthulhu game.

Dungeon Crawl Classics and Mutant Crawl Classics

Dungeon Crawl Classics and Mutant Crawl Classics are new interpretations of venerable classics published by Goodman Games. Good humor and fun are a big part of what they offer.

2300 AD

Based on one of the most venerable Sci-Fi RPGs around (Traveller - 1977), this revival eschews a far future setting for a closer and more familiar future with all the problems and possibilities that entails.

Wanderhome

A story telling game reminiscent of the Wind in the Willows. This is unlike any game I've played before, but it has a lovely, calming feel to it. Players start with playbooks that provide ideas of how to roleplay their characters. It's all improv from there. And there's no dice. The players determine everything that happens with their imagination.

Frontier Space

A new take inspired by Star Frontiers published by TSR in the 1980s.

Star Trek Adventures

Cinematic style role playing game covering all of the televised eras of Star Trek.

The Expanse

Based in equal parts on the series of novels from James S. A. Corey and the Amazon TV series inspired by it.
